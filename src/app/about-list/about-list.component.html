FAQ
  • Where are my old events?
    They are removed from the calander once the day expires.
  • How to update ?
    Click on an event and start typing.
  • How to delete ?
    Click on an event and delete.
  • Can i drag an event to another calander day ?
    Yes, Click on an event and drag it to another calander day.
  • Can i make copies of the same event and add the event to another calander day?
    Yes,Drag the event while pressing the ctrl key.
